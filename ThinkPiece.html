Critical thinking and Bayesian statistics

Critical thinking and Bayesian statistics are talking about the same thing. In social sciences, we emphasize the importance of recognizing and challenging assumptions and biases. Similarly, in Bayesian statistics, we talk about conditional probability and prior distribution. That's why it made sense for me to double major in political economy and statistics.

Reading and Webscraping

Structure is everything. If you know the layout of an essay, you can grasp its ideas more quickly. If you know the layout of a website, writing a webscraping script is less of a pain. Write well-structured essays and make well-structured websites!
